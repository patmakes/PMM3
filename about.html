Who I Am

I'm a multi-instrumentalist, producer, and technology enthusiast from Toronto, ON. I've been playing music for almost 20 years, covering Classical, Jazz, Rock, Blues, and Electronic music in a variety of Countries, Institutions, and Establishments. These days I'm spending my time still enjoying my love of music, crafting my influences over the years into a unique sound.
