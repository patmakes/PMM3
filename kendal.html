Kendal Thompson

"I grew up in Alliston, Ontario Canada, just outside of Toronto. I wanted nothing more than to move to the city and share my talent; to sing, write and perform for a living. Like most, my traumas and personal struggles came with me. Suffering from stage fright and extreme self doubt my road to success seemed distant and unattainable. I decided that there was a deep healing that needed to happen in order for me to experience the life I dreamed of. After doing an immense amount of inner work my purpose has now grown. I want to help others realize their own healing powers and strength so they too can feel free to share and embrace who they are and what they do as well. -as you listen to my music I hope I help you feel this way."
