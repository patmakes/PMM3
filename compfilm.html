Composing for Film

Since 2012, I have enjoyed producing music and sounds for all different kinds of media. You can find some examples of my work in the aside. One of my favourite artists to compose for over the years has been Matthew Del Degan, the creator of Lovebot. We worked together to give the music for his kickstarter campaign the futuristic yet comforting aesthetic of his cute creation. In another vein, Will Bowes' Hunting Season is a suspenseful ride that offers a beautiful look at Northern Ontario and was a very exciting challenge to score. It features some of my darkest experimental string sounds and textures. If you have need of scoring or sound design in your next project, feel free to contact me below.
